ABOUT US

The Software Analysis and Intelligence Lab is one of the key laboratories of Tianjin University, which is dedicated to the field of software analysis and has in-depth research in the fields of software testing, artificial intelligence testing, autonomous driving, and compiler testing. Our lab has an experienced team of teachers and serious and eager students, with deep theoretical foundation and rich scientific research experience. Our lab closely follows the international academic frontiers, actively launches project cooperation with many companies, promotes the industrialization of scientific and technological achievements, and jointly serves the construction of an innovative country, and provides a Tianjin University's solution for intelligent China.

Shuang Liu (刘爽)

Associate Professor

Shuang Liu is currently an associate professor at College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU). She worked in Singapore Institute of Technology (SiT) as a lecturer before joining TJU. Shuang received her PhD degree from National University of Singapore and her Bachelor of Honor from Renmin University. Shuang’s research interests include program understanding, anomaly detection and testing of software and CPS systems. She has published around 20 papers on top-tier international conference and journals, including TSE, ESEC/FSE, ASE and FM. She serves as program committee members of multiple international conferences and reviewers of multiple international journals.

Research interests:

Mainly focus on Program understanding, anomaly detection, concurrent program bug detection and fixing, testing and verification of machine learning algorithms

Junjie Chen（陈俊洁）

Associate Professor

Junjie Chen is a Tenured Associate Professor, the leader of the Software Engineering team in College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. He received his PhD degree (2019) from Peking University (China) under the supervison of Lu Zhang, Bing Xie, Dan Hao, and Yingfei Xiong. He won the CCF Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award (2019, the highest award for PhD graduate in China with less than 10 awards for all disciplines of computer science per year). He has published over 40 research papers, winning three ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Awards (FSE 2020, ASE 2019, ISSTA 2019). His research has helped detect hundreds of bugs for Compilers and VMs (GCC, LLVM, OpenJ9, TVM), open-source projects from GitHub.

Research interests:

Intelligent Software Testing and Analysis, including compiler testing, AI for testing, testing for AI, automated debugging, AIOps.

Jiajun Jiang（姜佳君）

Associate Research Professor (Tenure-track)

Jiajun Jiang is currently a tenure-track associate professor (副研究员) at College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. He received his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from Peking University (Beijing, China), and his Bachelor's degree in 2015 from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Xi'an, China). His research interests lie in Automated Software Debugging and Program Transformation, especially focus on Automated Program Repair, Fault Localization, Bug Detection, Automated API Migration, and Systematic Program Editing.

Research interests:

Automated Software Debugging and Program Transformation: Mainly focusing on automated program repair, fault localization and bug detection, automated API migration and systematic program editing.
